About the Parambulator

The parambulator is an application that allows for the existance of the Cloud Ark and Izzy. Due to the large amount of arklets and bolides in the orbit of Earth, collisions are imminent. The parambulator gives a visual representation of the location of each arklet and if its in danger of collision or not.

If an arklet is in danger of collision, it will be highlighted red. The arklet's AI will then kick in and try to avoid the collision. As well as avoidance, the arklet AI also strives to keep the arklets near Izzy for refueling and social purposes.

Simulator GUI Controls

Spawn Arklets
Creates arklets in the scene

Number of Arklets
Determines the number of Arklets to be spawned by Spawn Arklets

Spawn Bolides
Continuously spawns bolides into the scene

Frequency
Determines the rate at which bolides will spawn into the scene

Size
Determines the size of the bolides entering the scene

a
Sets the Semimajor Axis of the orbit of the bolides entering the scene. For circular orbits, the semimajor axis is the distance between the centers of the bodies.

e
Sets the Eccentricity of the orbit of the bolides entering the scene. This is how much it is elongated compared to a circle.

i
Sets the Inclination of the orbit of the bolides entering the scene. This is the vertical tilt of the ellipse with respect to the reference plane.

O
Sets the Longitute of the Ascending Node of the orbit of the bolides entering the scene. this horizontally orietns the Ascending Node of the orbit.

o
Sets the Argument of Periapsis of the orbit of the bolides entering the scene. This defines the orientation of the ellipse in the orbital plane as an angle measured from the asending node to the periapsis.
